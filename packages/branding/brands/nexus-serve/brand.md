# Nexus Serve — Brand Specification

## Identity

**Full Name:** Nexus Serve
**Type:** Point-of-Sale & Employee Management System
**Parent:** Realms & Beyond
**Audience:** Restaurant managers, operations staff, business owners

---

## Logo

| Asset | File | Usage |
|-------|------|-------|
| Icon / Favicon | `logo-icon.svg` | App icons, favicons, dashboards |
| Full Wordmark | `logo-full.svg` | App header, login screen, documents |
| Social Banner | `banner.svg` | Open Graph, presentations, onboarding |

**Logo motif:** A central hexagon (representing the core system / command centre) connected to six satellite nodes by clean lines — a network topology. The hexagon suggests precision, interconnection, and a technical command hub.

| Tier | Font | Use |
|------|------|-----|

| Data / Mono | Share Tech Mono | Order IDs, timestamps, terminal values, live data |
| Body | Inter (or EB Garamond) | Instructions, descriptions, onboarding copy |

---

## Voice & Tone

- **Tone:** Professional, clear, reassuring — the voice of a system that runs reliably
- **Not:** Playful, overly casual, or marketing-heavy in operational contexts
- **Style:** Direct, action-oriented. Every word earns its place. Favour verbs in UI copy.
- **Examples:** "Manage staff" not "People & HR", "Open register" not "Begin session"

## Usage Rules

- The hexagon + satellite node structure must always be rendered as an intact unit
- Do not use the ByteBurger palette in Nexus Serve — these are operationally separate contexts
- Teal blue `#2a7abf` is the primary trust colour — use it for primary actions, not amber
- The wordmark "NEXUS" is always in ice white `#e4f0f8`; "SERVE" is always in mint teal `#4abfab`
- Data displays should favour monospace (Share Tech Mono) for readability and alignment
- Border radius: 4px standard; 2px for data tables and terminal-style elements
