# Realms & Beyond — Brand Specification

## Identity

**Full Name:** Realms & Beyond
**Type:** Private Holding Company
**Scope:** Multi-product technology ecosystem spanning TTRPG, food tech, and operations software

---

## Logo

| Asset | File | Usage |
|-------|------|-------|
| Icon / Favicon | `logo-icon.svg` | App icons, favicons, small contexts |
| Full Wordmark | `logo-full.svg` | Headers, presentations, documents |
| Social Banner | `banner.svg` | Open Graph, social media, cover images |

**Logo motif:** A gateway arch with stars visible through the opening — representing the core concept of crossing into other realms and building beyond the ordinary.

**Clear space:** Maintain padding equal to the arch width on all sides of the icon.
**Minimum size:** 24px height for full logo; 16px for icon-only.

## Colour Palette

### Primary

| Role | Name | Hex | Use |
|------|------|-----|-----|
| Background Dark | Warm Charcoal | `#262425` | Page backgrounds, card surfaces |
| Background Light | Neutral Parchment | `#f4f3f1` | Light mode backgrounds |
| Primary | Sage Olive | `#8c9657` | Primary actions, active states, icons |
| Accent | Amber Moss | `#a87a3a` | Logo accents, highlights, headings |
| Text Dark | Warm Parchment | `#f0e5d9` | Body text on dark backgrounds |
| Text Light | Near Black | `#2a2729` | Body text on light backgrounds |

### Support

| Role | Hex |
|------|-----|
| Danger | `#a84a3a` |
| Warning | `#c2a84a` |
| Success | `#6c8a4a` |
| Info / Submit | `#4a6c8a` |

### Do Not Use
- Neon or highly saturated colours
- Cold blue-black palettes
- Bright primary colours (red, green, blue at full saturation)

## Typography

| Tier | Font | Fallback | Use |
|------|------|----------|-----|
| Display | Cinzel | Palatino Linotype, Georgia, serif | Hero titles, section headings, logo wordmark |
| Body | EB Garamond | Georgia, serif | Long-form copy, editorial text |
| UI / Labels | Barlow Condensed | Arial Narrow, sans-serif | Navigation, tags, HUD labels, sub-taglines |
| Mono | Share Tech Mono | Courier New, monospace | Code, data readouts, technical text |

---

## Voice & Tone

- **Tone:** Measured, curious, visionary — the voice of a company that builds worlds
- **Not:** Corporate-cold, hype-driven, overly casual
- **Style:** Short, declarative sentences. Lean on nouns. Avoid filler adjectives.

## Usage Rules

- The logo **must never** be placed on a background lighter than `#1e1d1c` without switching to the light-mode variant
- Do **not** recolour the amber arch in the icon — it is the signature brand element
- The wordmark "BEYOND" is always rendered in `#a87a3a` (amber) to reinforce the brand hierarchy
- Scanline overlays (subtle CRT texture) are part of the R&B signature — include them where appropriate
