# Aetherscribe — Brand Specification

## Identity

**Full Name:** Aetherscribe
**Type:** TTRPG Content Creation & Campaign Management Platform
**Parent:** Realms & Beyond
**Audience:** Game masters, worldbuilders, tabletop RPG creators

---

## Logo

| Asset | File | Usage |
|-------|------|-------|
| Icon / Favicon | `logo-icon.svg` | App icons, favicons, small contexts |
| Full Wordmark | `logo-full.svg` | App header, auth screen, documents |
| Social Banner | `banner.svg` | Open Graph, social media, splash images |

**Logo motif:** A feather quill positioned diagonally within an arcane rune circle, with an amber ink nib at the tip. The quill represents the act of world-creation through writing; the circle suggests the infinite, self-contained nature of a well-built world.

## Voice & Tone

- **Tone:** Mystical but precise — like an ancient archivist with deep knowledge
- **Not:** Playful or casual; not sci-fi clinical
- **Vocabulary:** Favours: "inscribe", "chronicle", "codex", "realm", "lore", "forge", "world"
- **Style:** Evocative, confident. Short phrases for UI, longer prose for documentation.

## Usage Rules

- The quill and rune circle must always appear together as one unit in the icon
- The amber ink nib is the focal point — it must never be removed or recoloured
- The logo works on dark backgrounds only; for light mode use a version with sage on `#f4f3f1`
- "AETHERSCRIBE" in the wordmark must always be one word, in Cinzel, in parchment `#f0e5d9`
- "INSCRIBE INFINITY" sub-tagline is always in Barlow Condensed uppercase, amber `#a87a3a`
